Me elsewhere

Erdős number

My Erdős number is 4:

  • Me — Rajeev Alur — Scott Weinstein — Saharon Shelah — Paul Erdős

There are at least 3 other paths (and probably many more), but I like this one because I already knew (or knew of) everyone in the path.

Academic lineage

  • Me — Rajeev Alur — David Dill — Edmund Clarke — Robert Constable — Stephen Kleene — Alonzo Church

At this point the lineage diverges from computer science into math: to Oswald Veblen, then E. H. Moore, then H. A. Newton, then Michel Chasles, then Siméon Poisson, who had two advisors, Joseph-Louis Lagrange and Pierre-Simon Laplace.

The full genealogy can be explored here.
